A Guide to Public Restrooms in San Francisco










About

This is a friendly guide to those who are not familiar with San Francisco and it's lack of public restrooms. It's important to note that there are approximately 9,784 homeless people "living" in San Francisco and millions of tourists traveling every year; however, there are only 25 public restrooms in San Francisco (excluding government buildings and other businesses). Not to mention the millions of people who already lives here. This is a huge problem because a lot of people do not have access to restrooms due to them always being crowded, dirty, or out-of-service. Public restrooms is a human necessity and we need to stand together and make more bathrooms. This is our city and our voices need to be heard!
